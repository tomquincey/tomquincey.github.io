About this site

This is a baking website for people who know nothing about baking.

It turns out most simple recipes only require a combination of 4 main ingredients; flour, butter, sugar and eggs - though you should keep some water and milk handy too.

The rings on each baked good represent the ratios of the masses of ingredients used to make it.
